HISTORY OF THE COMPANY


Mr. Deomel M. Lucban started from an employee to a business owner. In 2010, he grabbed the opportunity to be part of the company of his mentor Mr. Mike Mantal. Equipped with the tenacity and expertise, he established his own company – an accounting service provider on February 2015. DML Business Solutions Co. started as a sole proprietorship; DML Accounting Services.

The company was spearheaded by Mr. Deomel M. Lucban with Ms. Angelie A. Gubot as his Administrative Officer, located at Cogeo, Antipolo City. DML Accounting Services had its first branch in Quezon City under the trade name of AEGLE RRAD Business Solution.
From two people as manpower, force of the business grows into staggering 13 persons separated into 4 branches as of this date: The Cogeo, Pampanga, Pasig and Commonwealth branches, 2019 from sole proprietorship changed its ownership into partnership.

As a promising business solution provider, it expanded its line of services from Business Registration, Tax Filing, Printing of Receipt, and Bookkeeping to Payroll processing, Human Resource Outsourcing, and P.C.A.B. Registration.

ABOUT THE LOGO


Round Shape
It symbolizes our company that works with unity, has commitment with clients and serves love to the community.


Color
The yellow color represents warmth welcome to our clients as well as to our prospective clients. The red, green and white color represents the Chairman’s faith to his religion, the Iglesia ni Cristo.


3 Stars
It symbolizes the three main land of the Philippines; Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao. DML aims to serve not only the Metro but also nationwide.


3 Curves
It represents the 3 founding partners of DML when it was started.


Flame
It symbolizes “the light of spirit, knowledge, and life of every business in the community.”
